FAQ

Common questions

Is this an auto-trader?

No. Tradecontrollers is an execution control layer (a keypad) that helps you place and manage orders deliberately.

Will I become profitable?

No. Tradecontrollers may improve execution discipline, but trading is risky. Use risk capital only. Losses may exceed the initial investment. Past results do not predict future outcomes.

What problem does it solve?

Accidental clicks, inconsistent bracket placement, rushed risk changes, and overtrading spirals.

Will it work with my prop firm?

If your prop firm supports Tradovate or NinjaTrader, Tradecontrollers is designed to fit that environment.

How do I install?

Tradecontrollers works on any laptop or desktop that supports Chrome extensions. After purchase, you’ll receive setup steps and an access key by email.

It’s not opening the extension.

Ensure a supported trading environment is open (TradingView in-browser, or NinjaTrader/Tradovate web). If the popup is blocked, allow popups for the site and try again.

It’s asking me for an access key.

This is expected. Check your email after purchase for the access key. If you need help, open a ticket in Discord.

Is it affiliated with NinjaTrader or Tradovate?

No. Tradecontrollers is independent and is not endorsed by NinjaTrader, Tradovate, or any prop firms.
