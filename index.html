FAQ

Common questions

Is this an auto-trader?

No. TradeControllers is an execution control layer (a keypad) that helps you place and manage orders deliberately.

What problem does it solve?

Accidental clicks, inconsistent bracket placement, rushed risk changes, and overtrading spirals. The keypad turns those into repeatable, guarded actions.

Will it work with my prop firm?

If your prop firm supports Tradovate or NinjaTrader workflows, TradeControllers is designed to fit that execution environment.

How do I install?

Tradecontrollers works on any laptop or desktop that offers chrome extensions. We recommend Arc browser since you can move the pad around.

It's asking me for access key?

This is intentional, check your email for the access key! If you need any assistance create a ticket on discord!
